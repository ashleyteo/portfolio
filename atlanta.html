Atlanta Airport Review

The goal of this project was to review the performance of the Hartfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL) for any areas of improvement. The data included 59 columns and 22,537,772 rows before being cleaned and loaded into Tableau.

To get a feel for the data and provide overall metrics, a high level Tableau dashboard was created.

This was then supplemented with detailed analysis of performance specific data (delays and cancellations) and it was discovered that flight delays were highest during the early morning hours (3 - 6 AM), when very few flights were scheduled to fly.

This finding suggests that early morning delays are not handled efficiently and primarily consists of United and Frontier Airlines, who have the largest number of unscheduled flights leaving between midnight and 6AM.

Overall, the Hartfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL) needs to focus on overnight staffing to improve its performance.
